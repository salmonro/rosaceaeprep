About Rosaceae Prep

Rosaceae Prep is a free preparation website for the USA Biology Olympiad (USABO) and AP Biology.

Questions are written by Rosa Lee, a junior at Cupertino High School. She is a past USABO semifinalist. The question bank is made to emulate real USABO-style questions and College Board-style questions.

Interested in joining the team? Join the Discord server and message Rosa (@argi9)!
