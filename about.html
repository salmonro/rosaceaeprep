About Rosaceae Prep

Rosaceae Prep is a free preparation website for the USA Biology Olympiad (USABO) and AP Biology. Questions are written by Rosa Lee, a high school junior at Cupertino High School. She is a past USABO semifinalist. The question bank is made to emulate real USABO-style questions and College Board style questions. Interested in joining?
